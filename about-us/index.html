Company Overview

Ryss Lab is a premier contract research organization (CRO) offering chemistry services for the biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries. Since 1997, we have been serving businesses and organizations worldwide from our headquarters in the Union City, California and research facilities in Taipei, Taiwan. Our dedication to upholding the highest professional and scientific standards, along with our enthusiasm for delivering the best results, make us the partner of choice from budding biotech startups to multinational pharmaceuticals.

Ryss Lab provides clients with synthesis and manufacturing services and research and development (R&D) based on a per-project or full time equivalent (FTE) fee.

Mission and Values

Ryss Lab is committed to achieving excellent results for challenging projects in a timely and cost-effective manner through the close cooperation of clients and talented scientists; to providing quick and friendly support for clients in need; to quality check all finished products to ensure complete customer satisfication.
